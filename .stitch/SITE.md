# FoodSharePoint Site Vision

FoodSharePoint is a vibrant Nigerian food sharing marketplace. It connects vendors (cooks) with community members to share delicious, affordable meals through "SharePoints".

## 1. Vision
- **Vibrant & Earthy**: A lush, rustic "Farm to Table" aesthetic that feels warm and community-driven.
- **Community-Driven**: Strong emphasis on trust, local roots, and sharing.
- **Nigerian Marketplace**: Authentic and energetic representation of Nigerian food culture and markets.
